Ten Traditional Art of Myanmar


Ten traditional arts, called pan sè myo ('Ten Flowers'), were established within the culture. The ten arts are asfollows:Lacquerware is also a distinct craft originating from the late Bagan period with the earliest fragments of basketry in Bagan being dated to the 13th century.

  • Blacksmith (ပန်းပဲ ba-be)

  • Woodcarving (ပန်းပု ba-bu)

  • Goldsmith (ပန်းထိမ် ba-dein)

  • Stucco relief (ပန်းတော့ pandaw)

  • Masonry (ပန်းရန် pa-yan)

  • Stone carving (ပန်းတမော့ pantamaw)

  • Turnery (ပန်းပွတ် panbut)

  • Painting (ပန်းချီ bagyi)

  • Lacquerware (ပန်းယွန် panyum)

  • Bronze casting (ပန်းတဉ် badin)
